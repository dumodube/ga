welcome
Amplifying Youth Voices

Digital Advocate Int. is a Pan-African, action-oriented, youth-led organization that works towards advancing youth participation, development and leadership through advocacy, information sharing, livelihoods and transformative education. We aspire to enhance respect of all citizen’s rights, promoting the values of ubuntu, unity, self-determination, integrity and accountability. The organization was formed in October 2018 with the aim to:

  • Engage the governments, parliamentarians and youth oriented civil society organizations in Southern Africa.
  • Influence youth and communities’ perception and public opinion on health and social issues including gender.
  • Promote youth-led, citizen journalism on issues that matter.
  • Advocate for the equitable access to socio-economic and health services by young people.
